The British Hedgehog Preservation Society
Project OverviewThe British Hedgehog Preservation Society, is a small local charity dedicated to the preservation of British hedgehog population. This was our first choice due to their work and history, however we came across some design issues such as the lacking in consistency in icon quality, the donate button was not focused or prioritised and also a weak alignment and the structure of the page was both overwhelming and confusing. We then, thought that this website would not effectively satisfy the needs of the user, and in order to validate this, we had to carry out research ro understand our user's needs.

National Security Association - NSA
Project Overview
User problem - Job seekers in cybersecurity often face a UX challenge when navigating job websites such as, the NSA career webpage with complicated or overwhelming application processes, involving extensive clicking and page navigation. This frustrating experience can discourage candidates, highlighting the need for simplified processes that is satisfying to professionals preference for efficiency and user-friendliness. Solution - Introducing a simplified job application portal tailored for cybersecurity professionals, offering a straightforward and user-friendly experience. This platform compiles all necessary information onto a single page, utilizing continuous scroll navigation for seamless access to credentials, responsibilities, and application guidelines.
